Frequently Asked Questions

This is a "living document". We will update and elaborate it with questions and clarifications as and when they arise. Created: 9th April 2025

When is Alone: Elysium taking place?
Friday, 27th Feb – Sunday, 1st March, 2026.
Where will Alone: Elysium take place?
Youlbury Scout camp, Youlbury, Boars Hill, Oxford OX1 5HD
How do I book for Alone: Elysium?
Go to this sign up form here.
How much does Alone: Elysium cost?
We have four types of ticket available:
  • Concession - £200 (TBD)
  • Standard Price - £300
  • Supporter Tier 1 - £350 (goodies to be announced)
  • Supporter tier 2 - £450 (extra goodies to be announced)

We will have options for people to pay in instalments

Who's behind Alone: Elysium?
Flowstate. A group of people with long experience in running events, telling stories and being geeks.
Is Alone: Elysium suitable for someone of my disposition?
That's for you to decide; please refer to the content warning for more information.
What do I need to bring?
TBD as we reveal more about character types. However...simple and warm is good. Kit will not require substantial investment in order to be immersive.
Is the event catered?
Yes, please indicate any specific dietary requirements when booking.
How many players will there be for Alone: Elysium?
A maximum of 90 players will be permitted.
What time of day does Alone: Discord start?
Time in 24hr, from 8.30pm on Friday, through until approximately 3am Sunday. All players to leave the site by lunchtime on Sunday.
Where can I park?
There is free, secure parking on site.
What are the sleeping arrangements?
Communal, indoor bunkbeds. There is some camping for those who want it but it will be OOC only.
What facilities are there at the venue?
There are toilets and showers.
