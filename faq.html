Frequently Asked Questions

This is a "living document". We will update and elaborate it with questions and clarifications as and when they arise. Created: 31st January, 2020, updated: 2nd May, 2021

When is Alone: Discord taking place?
Friday, 12th – Sunday, 14th November, 2021.
Where will Alone: Discord take place?
The Gaol Events, Unit 9, Oakham Enterprise Park, Burley, Oakham, Rutland, LE15 7TU
How do I book for Alone: Discord?
Email us on mail@alonediscord.com.
How much does Alone: Discord cost?
£175 in total, with 50% (£87.50) on booking. The final £87.50 is due by 30th September 2021.
Who's behind Alone: Discord?
Flowstate. A group of people with long experience in running events, telling stories and being geeks.
Is Alone: Discord suitable for someone of my disposition?
That's for you to decide; please refer to the content warning for more information.

TBD as we reveal more about character types. However...simple and warm is good. Kit will not require substantial investment in order to be immersive.
Is the event catered?
Yes, please indicate any specific dietary requirements when booking.
How many players will there be for Alone: Discord?
A maximum of 45 players will be permitted.
What time of day does Alone: Discord start?
Time in 24hr, from 8.30pm on Friday, through until approximately 3am Sunday. All players to leave the site by lunchtime on Sunday.
Where can I park?
There is free, secure parking on site.
What are the sleeping arrangements?
Communal, indoor bunkbeds. There is no camping.
What facilities are there at the venue?
There are toilets but no showers.
